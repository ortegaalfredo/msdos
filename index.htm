MS-DOS v 1.0

Microsoft Denial of Service ;)

 

What is MSDOS?

It's a distribution of 50+ Denial of Service attacks that i found in the net, I arranged them and put a easy configurable graphical interface. Think like a Linux distribution, but with DOS attacks...
The victim of the attacks are usually win95, win98,win98sr2 and winNT4 boxes, but may work with other systems too.
The main objetive is to remotely hang the box.
 


Requeriments

MSDOS requieres the following packages:
 

  • cdialog-0.9a  for the console interface

  • gnome-utils-1.4.0 for the gnome interface

  • nmap 2.0+ for port scanning

May work with older versiones, but i only test it with this ones.

MSDOS work out-of-the-box with Mandrake 8.0 (gnome workstation),
and maybe with redhat 7.1 too.
 


Compilation
The distribution comes with pre-compiled binaries for linux-glibc 2.1 .if they don't work or you have a different system, go to the ./src dir
and ejecute ./compile.sh .The generated binaries must then be moved to the ./bin directory.
